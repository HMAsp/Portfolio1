Inside the trip | project exam

To conclude our first year journey we had the project exam. This travelblog binds it all together and showcases my acquired skills in design, development and programming. This project had us create a blog. About me

Hi Again! Like I started out saying, my name is Hans or in some circles more commonly known as Spookyrumble. I have been a gamer most of my life so as you probably guessed, Spookyrumble is my gamer name going back to the release of the XBox 360 when I started playing FPS games and I needed a tag. I occationally stream on Twitch under the same name and I am a part of many communities all over the world.


Personally I am a very calm and collected person. I work on a very logical level (to me) and I guess you can call me an observer and thinker more than a talker/speaker. I prefer to not take up to much space in a room but I will voice up if I feel the need and that is usually if I think someone could need help in some way.


Most of my days are spent on my daughters. August 2023 they are all in school as my 5 year old starts 1st grade, which is nice. Taking this time right now to do these studies have been pure gold and I am in a very happy place. Biggest dream is to find some awesome collagues after I graduate so I can keep learning. I am enjoying development a lot as it speaks to my need to know how things work but also the arts and crafts it holds. There are barely any limits to what we can do!

After having worked in the car industry more or less since 2003 with everything from the floor as a mechanic and car detailer to the parts department but mostly in the customer service department I found myself wanting to pursue a dream of doing something I found very interesting and fun. Like I said before, I have always been a gamer and very much interested in tech I wanted to go the IT way and started researching what branch I thought would suit me best.

The choice landed on Front-End Development because it speaks to my natural qualities. I very much enjoy creating, troubleshooting and just making things work. I am one of those kids who picked apart my night stand radio just to see what it looked like inside. I am absolutely loving learning about development and working on these projects figuring out how to do what I want to do is so much fun and I feel like this is the perfect match for me. I am only sorry I did not start this sooner but while this waited I have had a long good life this far. Full of experience and my three wonderful daughters and my wife. May the rest of it be filled with awesome projects and creative people to work with!
